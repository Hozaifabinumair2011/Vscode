Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth," is a region renowned for its breathtaking beauty, including snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and serene lakes. 
The Kashmir Valley, nestled within the Himalayas, is known for its fertile land and the Jhelum River, a major Himalayan river that flows through it.
The region is home to a variety of wildlife, including the endangered Kashmir stag (Hangul) and black bears, protected within sanctuaries like Dachigam National Park. Kashmir also boasts diverse flora, including numerous wild flower species and the largest Tulip Garden in Asia.
